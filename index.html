Bruno Chaihuaque is an Adjunct Professor at Department of Management Sciences at the Pontificia Universidad Catolica del Peru. He is currently a PhD student in Strategic Management and holds a Master's degree in Business Administration at the CENTRUM Graduate Business School, a Master in International Business Management at Universidad Complutense de Madrid, and a Diploma in Applied Statistics at the Pontificia Universidad Catolica del Peru. He works as a specialist in Budget Programs at the Ministry of Economy and Finance of Peru.

His primary research interests include organizational development and growth, particularly knowledge management and innovation; as well as informal enterprises and indigenous entrepreneurship
